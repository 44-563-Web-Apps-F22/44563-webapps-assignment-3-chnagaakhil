Fun Facts about Rhinos

  1. The name rhinoceros means ‘nose horn’ and its often shortened to rhino
  2. These incredible creatures are some of the biggest animals in the world
  3. They can grow to 1.8m tall and can weigh a massive 2,500kg
  4. Rhinos are herbivores and love to munch on grass, plants and bushes!
  5. A group of rhino is called a ‘crash’
