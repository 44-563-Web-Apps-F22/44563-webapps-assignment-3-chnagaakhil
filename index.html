National History Museum

A National History Museum is a history museum dedicated to presenting artifacts and exhibits reflecting the history of a particular nation, usually its home country.
The earliest public museums,The Ashmolean Museum in Oxford and The Louvre Museum in Paris, were focused on natural history and art, respectively.The National
Museum of Natural History was opened its doors on March 17, 1910 , it housed art, culture, history, and natural history collections in Washington, D.C.
