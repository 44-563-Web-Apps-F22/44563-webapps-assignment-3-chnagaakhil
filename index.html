National History Museum

A National History Museum is a history museum dedicated to presenting artifacts and exhibits reflecting the history of a particular nation, usually its home country.
The earliest public museums,The Ashmolean Museum in Oxford and The Louvre Museum in Paris, were focused on natural history and art, respectively.The National
Museum of Natural History was opened its doors on March 17, 1910 , it housed art, culture, history, and natural history collections in Washington, D.C.
One of the famous exhibit in this museum is David H. Koch Hall of Fossils - Deep Time in First Floor Sculpture of T-Rex which is around 4.6 billion years of time.
Secondly, the Eternal Life in Ancient Egypt relating the tombs of ancients egypt gods which is around 2000 years old. Finally, the Game Changer of Technology in
Genetics - The Genome The genome contains the instructional code of every living thing. Learning about the human genome will revolutionize your thinking about
your biology, your health, and our place as humans in the natural world.
